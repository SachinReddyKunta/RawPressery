About The Juice

Existing packaged juices in the market contain preservatives, chemicals, stabilizers and are full of added sugar. While our juices are made of 100% raw fruits and vegetables, tetrapak juices in the market typically contain only 8-10% fruit or vegetables.

RAW Pressery juices are not pasteurized at high temperatures and contain no chemicals, preservatives, artificial colours or added sugar. In addition, household juicers or blenders involve a high-speed rotating blade or strainer, which create heat and cause oxidation, destroying vital nutrients in the juice.

As no heat or air is thrust through the juice during the cold-pressing process, it retains far more nutrition and living enzymes are kept intact. This allows you to absorb the maximum nutrients, vitamins and minerals possible from the raw produce.

Just like Natural Food Products which do not have any preservatives; our Juices are meant for Immediate Consumption and are best enjoyed within 2-3 hours of opening (if not refrigerated). Refrigeration helps you enjoy the juice up to 72 hours.

Cold-Pressed Juicing retains significant amount of fibre from Fruits & Vegetables. Therefore, sometimes the heavier components, like fibre tend to settle down causing sedimentation at the bottom portion of the bottle. There’s no need to worry, just Shake and Take.

Our juices contain plenty of soluble fibre as well as an abundance of vitamins, minerals and trace amount of insoluble fibre.

High Pressure Processing (HPP) is a process in which already sealed juices are introduced into a vessel and are subjected to a high level of water pressure that eliminate harmful bacteria without altering the flavour, texture or nutritional value of the juices and thereby naturally extends its shelf life to 45 days.
